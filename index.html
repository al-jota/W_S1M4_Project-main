Overview

John Ronald Reuel Tolkien lived from 3 January 1892 - 2 September 1973. He was an English writer, well known for works such as The Hobbit and The Lord of the Rings.

Personal Life

Tolkien was married to Edith Bratt and together they had four children.

Writing

Tolkien wrote many works, but is perhaps best known for:

  • The Hobbit
  • The Lord of the Rings
    • The Fellowship of the Ring
    • The Two Towers
    • The Return of the King
